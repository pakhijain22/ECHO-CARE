Medical Disclaimer

Important Safety Information - Please Read Carefully

🚨 CRITICAL NOTICE

EchoCare is NOT a medical device and does NOT provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

This application is a prototype tracking and organizational tool designed for educational and demonstration purposes only. It should never replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

What EchoCare Is

  • A symptom tracking tool that allows caregivers to log and monitor daily observations
  • An organizational aid to help caregivers maintain records of behavioral patterns and symptoms
  • A communication tool to help caregivers share documented observations with healthcare professionals
  • A prototype demonstration created for hackathon and educational purposes

What EchoCare Is NOT

  • NOT a medical device - EchoCare is not FDA-approved, cleared, or certified as a medical device
  • NOT a diagnostic tool - This application cannot and does not diagnose dementia, Alzheimer's disease, or any other medical condition
  • NOT a treatment provider - EchoCare does not provide medical treatment, therapy, or clinical interventions
  • NOT a substitute for professional care - This tool does not replace consultations with qualified healthcare providers
  • NOT an emergency service - EchoCare cannot detect or respond to medical emergencies
  • NOT AI-powered medical advice - Any insights or patterns displayed are rule-based observations, not artificial intelligence diagnoses

Your Responsibilities

Always Consult Licensed Healthcare Professionals
  • All medical decisions should be made in consultation with qualified, licensed healthcare providers
  • Never delay seeking professional medical advice based on information from this application
  • Always discuss any concerns about symptoms, medications, or treatment with a doctor
  • Bring your symptom logs to medical appointments as supplementary information only
Recognize Medical Emergencies
  • Call 112 immediately for life-threatening emergencies including but not limited to: severe chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke symptoms, loss of consciousness, severe bleeding, or head injuries
  • Do not rely on EchoCare alerts to identify emergencies - trust your judgment and err on the side of caution
  • Emergency symptoms require immediate medical attention regardless of what this application displays
Understand Limitations
  • Self-reported symptom tracking has inherent limitations and potential for error
  • Pattern insights are based on simple rules, not clinical assessment
  • This application cannot account for individual medical history, medications, or other health factors
  • Data accuracy depends entirely on the information you enter

Privacy & Data Security Notice

Prototype Status: This is a demonstration prototype that stores data locally in your browser using localStorage.

  • Data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any servers
  • Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all logged information
  • This prototype does not comply with HIPAA or other healthcare privacy regulations
  • Do not use this application for protected health information in a real healthcare setting
  • A production version would require proper security, encryption, and compliance measures
