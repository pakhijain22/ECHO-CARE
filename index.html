The Challenge

Caring for a loved one with dementia is overwhelming. Symptoms fluctuate daily, patterns are hard to track, and knowing when to seek medical help can be confusing.

Caregivers need a simple way to monitor changes, identify concerning trends, and communicate effectively with healthcare providers.

Our Solution

EchoCare is a caregiver-first web app that helps you:

  • Log daily symptoms in under 2 minutes
  • Visualize weekly trends and patterns
  • Receive rule-based alerts for red-flag symptoms
  • Connect with doctors and schedule appointments

You're not alone in this journey.

Key Features

📝
Daily Tracker

Log confusion, wandering, sleep issues, falls, and mood changes in seconds

Visual Insights
